Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 1, 2026  ·  Version: 1.0

ℹ️ Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains clearly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Introduction

Vanquish Inc. ("Vanquish," "we," "us," or "our") operates the AllergyGuard mobile application (the "App") and related services (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, share, and protect information about you when you use our Service.

By using AllergyGuard, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the Service.

This policy applies to all users of the AllergyGuard App, including users of the free tier and paid subscription plans.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Data Type What We Collect Why We Collect It
Account Information Name, email address, password (encrypted) To create and manage your account
Allergy Profiles Names, ages, and allergen lists for family members To personalize allergen analysis results
EpiPen Records Device labels, expiry dates, assigned profiles To send expiry reminder notifications
Scan History Menu text, dish names, analysis results To display your scan history within the app
Photos Photos of menus taken within the app To perform AI-based allergen analysis

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Data Type What We Collect Why We Collect It
Device Information Device type, operating system version, app version For technical support and app compatibility
Usage Data Features used, scan counts, session duration To improve the Service and enforce plan limits
Location Data Approximate GPS location when using restaurant detection To identify nearby restaurants and pull menus
Crash Reports Error logs and crash data To identify and fix technical issues

2.3 Health-Related Information

AllergyGuard collects food allergy information that may constitute health-related data. We treat this information with the highest level of care and do not share it with third parties for advertising, marketing, or any commercial purpose. This data is used solely to provide personalized allergen analysis within the Service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To operate the app, provide allergen analysis, manage allergy profiles, and deliver EpiPen expiry reminders
  • Account Management: To create, authenticate, and maintain your account
  • Personalization: To customize allergen analysis based on your saved family profiles and allergen preferences
  • AI Analysis: To send menu text, photo content, and allergen profile data to AI processing services for analysis
  • Notifications: To send EpiPen expiry alerts and optional meal scan reminders
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve app performance and features
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and resolve technical issues
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Service, subject to contractual obligations to protect your data:

  • Anthropic PBC: Menu text, allergen profile data, and food photos are transmitted to Anthropic's Claude AI for allergen analysis. Data is processed according to Anthropic's privacy practices.
  • Google LLC (Firebase): We use Google Firebase for user authentication, cloud data storage, and push notifications.
  • Google LLC (Places API): Approximate location data is shared with Google Places API for restaurant detection when you use the GPS feature.
  • Open Food Facts: Barcode numbers are submitted to the Open Food Facts open database to retrieve product ingredient information.
  • RevenueCat Inc.: Subscription purchase and management data is processed through RevenueCat. Payment card information is handled directly by the App Store or Google Play and is never transmitted to or stored by Vanquish Inc.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Vanquish Inc., our users, or the public.

4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or substantially all of our assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to the same privacy protections described in this policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers located in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to the transfer and storage of your data in the United States.

5.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Encrypted storage of account passwords
  • Firebase security rules restricting data access to authorized users only
  • Regular security assessments and updates

While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

5.3 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within thirty (30) days, except where retention is required by applicable law or for legitimate business purposes such as fraud prevention.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Correction

You may access and update your personal information at any time through the Settings section of the app. You may also contact us at support@allergyguard.io to request access to or correction of your personal data.

6.2 Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated personal data through the Settings section of the app. Upon confirmation, your data will be permanently deleted from our systems within thirty (30) days.

6.3 Notification Preferences

You may manage push notification preferences through your device's notification settings or through the Settings section of the app.

6.4 Location Data

Location access is optional. You may deny or revoke location permissions through your device settings at any time. Denying location access will disable the GPS restaurant detection feature but will not affect other features of the Service.

6.5 Camera and Photo Library

Camera and photo library access is requested only when you use the menu photo scanning feature. You may deny these permissions through your device settings, though doing so will disable photo-based menu scanning.

7. Children's Privacy

AllergyGuard is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. The app is designed to be used by parents and guardians on behalf of their children. Allergy profile data entered for children is associated with the parent or guardian's account.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, please contact us immediately at support@allergyguard.io and we will take steps to delete such information.

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: You may request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell personal information. You do not need to opt out of any sale of personal data.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights

To exercise your CCPA rights, please contact us at support@allergyguard.io.

9. International Users and GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or equivalent legislation, including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

Our legal basis for processing your personal data includes: (a) performance of our contract with you to provide the Service; (b) your consent where required; and (c) our legitimate interests in operating and improving the Service.

Continued use of the Service after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
