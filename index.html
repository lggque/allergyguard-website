Why we built this

AllergyGuardAI started with a moment no parent forgets.

Our son had an anaphylactic reaction at a catered family event — despite the caterer being told, in advance and clearly, about his peanut and tree nut allergies. EpiPen administered. 911 called. He's healthy today.

But we sat in that ambulance while he was being examined, and realized something: every allergy parent we know has lived through some version of this. The trust placed in strangers handling our child's food. The careful conversations that still go wrong. The vigilance that never gets to rest.

We built AllergyGuardAI so families with food allergies can eat anywhere with confidence — not just hope.

— Knowlton and Veronica, Vanquish Inc.

Features

What AllergyGuardAI does

Scan any restaurant menu. Point your camera at a menu or type a dish name. Every ingredient is checked against your family's specific allergies in seconds — safe, warn, or danger.

Scan any food label. Over three million products in our barcode database. Grocery shopping with confidence, not guesswork.

One app, your whole family. Individual profiles for each family member with their specific allergens. Switch between them instantly at any restaurant.

Allergy cards in 12 languages. Travel anywhere. Show your card at any table.

Waiter scripts, written for you. After every scan, get exactly what to say, what to ask, and what to watch for.

Community restaurant ratings. Rate restaurants based on your allergy experience and read what other allergy families share. The more families join, the safer everyone eats.

Emergency mode, one tap. Bold display of the user's name, severe allergens, and epinephrine device. "Read Aloud to Staff" for instant communication. One-tap to call 911 and directions to the nearest ER.

Epinephrine device expiry tracker. Automatic reminders at 60, 30, and 7 days for EpiPen, EpiPen Jr, Auvi-Q, Symjepi, Adrenaclick, and Neffy. Never let a life-saving device expire.
